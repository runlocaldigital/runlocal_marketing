About Run Local Marketing

RunLocal Marketing is a locally owned and dedicated digital marketing agency serving businesses throughout Longmont, Niwot, and Boulder, Colorado. With more than 15 years of experience in website design, SEO, web maintenance, and digital strategy, we help local companies build strong online foundations that attract customers and support long-term growth. Our mission is simple: create search-optimized, high-performing websites and marketing plans that help Colorado businesses stand out in an increasingly competitive digital landscape.

We specialize in designing SEO-friendly websites that are built to rank well on Google, load fast, and clearly communicate your brand. Every site is created with local search in mind, ensuring your business appears in relevant searches for the services you provide in the communities you serve. Our team handles everything from mobile-responsive design and content strategy to technical SEO elements that boost visibility and user experience.

Beyond website design, RunLocal Marketing offers comprehensive web maintenance and ongoing SEO services tailored to the needs of local small and midsized businesses. We manage updates, security, performance monitoring, keyword optimization, content improvements, citations, and Google Business Profile enhancements to keep your digital presence strong and competitive.

As a Colorado-based company, we understand the unique needs and values of businesses in the Longmont, Niwot, and Boulder area. We build long-term partnerships rooted in communication, transparency, and measurable results. Our digital marketing services—ranging from content creation to local advertising—are designed to drive targeted traffic, improve search rankings, and help you connect with customers right here in your community.

Whether you need a fresh website, stronger SEO, or an ongoing marketing partner, RunLocal Marketing provides the local expertise and proven strategies to help your business thrive.
